The UAE summer brings intense heat, a formidable adversary for even the most robust vehicles. While we all cherish the convenience of our cars, overlooking essential maintenance during these scorching months can swiftly lead to an unexpected breakdown. No one wants to be stranded on the roadside when the mercury soars — but taking proactive steps can significantly reduce the risk. Let's explore the vital car care tips that keep your vehicle reliable all season.

## 1. Protect your battery first

The heart of your car's electrical system faces immense strain in extreme temperatures and is often the first component to show signs of weakness. High temperatures accelerate the evaporation of battery fluid and can corrode internal components. Keep terminals clean and free of corrosion, periodically assess the charge level, and if your battery is nearing its typical 3–5 year lifespan, consider replacing it before summer truly sets in.

## 2. Don't neglect the cooling system

Your engine's temperature is meticulously regulated by coolant circulating through the radiator. An insufficient coolant level — or a compromised cooling system — can quickly lead to overheating, a highly damaging scenario for your engine. Keep the coolant reservoir topped up with the correct fluid, and have a professional inspect for leaks in the hoses or radiator.

## 3. Watch your tyre pressure

The relentless sun radiates heat onto the asphalt, which your tyres absorb. This can cause the air pressure inside to increase, potentially leading to overinflation or — more dangerously — a blowout if the tyre is already worn. Check tyre pressure weekly, ideally when the tyres are cold, and inspect tread depth and overall condition (including the spare) for wear, cracks or bulges.

## 4. Get your AC checked before you need it

If your AC isn't cooling as efficiently as it once did, it could indicate low refrigerant levels or other issues. A fully functional AC system not only keeps you comfortable but also reduces the strain on your engine that comes from trying to cool an overheated cabin. Getting your AC professionally checked — and recharged if necessary — is a small investment for a significant return in comfort and vehicle health.

## In conclusion

Preventing summer breakdowns in the UAE comes down to vigilant checks and timely maintenance.
