## Understanding the RTA inspection: what they look for

The RTA inspection, conducted at centres like Tasjeel, Shamil, or Wasel, is a thorough check to ensure your vehicle is safe and roadworthy. Here's a breakdown of the critical areas inspected.

### 1. Tyres — your car's foundation

**What they check:** worn-out tread depth, cracks on the sidewalls, uneven wear, and the manufacturing date. Tyres older than five years usually fail, even if they look okay.

**Oncall insight:** worn tyres are a major safety hazard, especially in the UAE heat and occasional rains. We can't replace tyres yet, but make sure yours are in top shape before the inspection to avoid a fail.

### 2. Lights & indicators — see and be seen

**What they check:** every single light — headlights (high and low beam), brake lights, indicators, hazard lights and taillights — must be fully functional. A single blown bulb can lead to a fail.

**Oncall solution:** often, a simple bulb change is all it takes. With our Car Light & Key Battery Service, we can check and replace faulty bulbs or fuses on the spot.

### 3. Battery & electricals — the heart of your car

**What they check:** the battery must be securely fastened, free from acid leaks, with clean terminals, and free of loose wiring or improper modifications.

**Oncall solution:** our Car Battery Service includes a pre-inspection health check. If your battery isn't up to par, a prompt car battery replacement can prevent an inspection failure and ensure your vehicle starts reliably every time.

### 4. Wipers & windshield — clear vision, clear roads

**What they check:** the windshield should be free of major cracks or chips that obstruct vision, wipers must work effectively, and the washer fluid system should be operational.

**Oncall insight:** small cracks can quickly become large ones in the UAE heat. While we don't repair glass, we'll make sure your wipers are in good condition for optimal visibility.

### 5. Engine & fluids — smooth running

**What they check:** visible leaks (oil, coolant), excessive engine noise, and whether the horn is working.

**Oncall solution:** our Mobile Oil Change service ensures your engine fluids are topped up and visible leaks are addressed, reducing the chances of an inspection snag.

## Your pre-inspection checklist: get ready to pass!

- **Clear fines** — about one month before renewal, settle all your traffic fines and Salik charges; your registration won't be renewed otherwise
- **Get insurance** — about one week before, ensure you have a valid 13-month insurance policy (mandatory for renewal)
- **Perform a DIY check** — a couple of days before, do a quick walk-around of lights, indicators, horn and wiper blades
- **Book your Oncall check** — let us handle your Car Battery Service, Car Light & Key Battery Service, and Mobile Oil Change before inspection day
- **Clean your car** — on the day of the test, make sure your vehicle is clean inside and out
