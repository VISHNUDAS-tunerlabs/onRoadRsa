## The warning signs: is your car AC losing its cool?

Before your AC completely gives up, it usually sends out a few distress signals — air that isn't as cold as it used to be, a strange hissing sound when the AC is on, or the cabin taking much longer to cool down. If you're experiencing any of these, it's highly likely your refrigerant levels are low.

Ignoring these signs can lead to bigger, more expensive problems. A low refrigerant level means your AC system has to work harder, putting strain on components and leading to premature wear and tear.
